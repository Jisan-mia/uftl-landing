How to Order Fuel


(Using Our Web App)

We have created a strong webapp to order fuel for your assets. The webapp is very simple and strong. You can order fuel very easily by following the steps below! You don’t need to follow the first two steps once you register.

1

Register

For setting up your account we need some of your info. You have to add your asset for the first time.

2

Add an Asset

Once you add your asset and register for the account you can simply select an asset from the asset list!

3

Place an Order

After completing the registration process you can simply order fuel by selecting an asset.

4

Get Fuel

We recieve your order at once. We will deliver the fuel in the specific time slot you selected!

Why Urgent Fuel?

We always try to provide the best services! You will get the following services once you register for an account. We are always trying to make our services better for you!

Affordable Transportation Cost

We take a very small amount of money as delivery charge per liter.

Set Your Own Schedule

You can set a specific time slot when you want to get the fuel for your asset.

100% Safe & Compliant Delivery

We use BRTA certified vehicle and trained fuel bowser to provide a safe delivery service!

Flexible Payment Option

We added maximum payment method to give you the full liberty to pay via any of the payment methods!

Increased Efficiency

Our delivery service can help you to save your time and can manage your asset’s fuel issue efficiently!

Eliminate Fuel Theft

We deliver fuel via our trusted fuel bowser to solve the fuel theft issue.

Central Billing & Reporting

Our webapp stores your usage data to show you the billing and reporting.

Simple & Strong Dashboard

With our webapp, we provide a strong dashboard where you can manage your account easily!
